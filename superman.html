Captain America

About Captain America

Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers, is a Marvel Comics superhero. He was a frail young man transformed into a super-soldier during World War II using the experimental Super-Soldier Serum. Wielding his iconic vibranium shield, he fought against the Axis powers and later became a symbol of freedom and justice.

Abilities and Powers

  • Superhuman Strength and Agility
  • Expert Hand-to-Hand Combatant
  • Master Shield Fighter
  • Enhanced Endurance and Reflexes
